Our Mission

Rise Above Recovery is dedicated to creating a structured, supportive environment where individuals overcoming addiction can rebuild their lives with dignity. Our mission is rooted in compassion, accountability, and community support — providing residents a safe place to grow, heal, and transition back into society. We believe that recovery is a journey best walked with others, and every step forward is worth celebrating.

Compassion

We meet everyone with empathy, understanding that each recovery story is unique.

Accountability

We build responsibility through structure, expectations, and daily support systems.

Community

We cultivate connection — no one recovers alone, and everyone belongs here.

What Makes Our Mission Different?

At Rise Above Recovery, our foundation is built not only on principles but on lived experience. Our team includes individuals who understand the challenges of addiction and the strength required to overcome it. We believe healing happens in community — with shared meals, group discussions, personal mentorship, and structured daily living.

Trauma-Informed Support

We recognize the deep roots of trauma and respond with intentional care, boundaries, and trust-building practices.

Life Skills Development

From cooking and cleaning to job-readiness and budgeting, we equip residents with practical tools for long-term success.

Peer Mentorship

Residents have the opportunity to mentor others in their recovery journey, reinforcing community and leadership.

Long-Term Vision

We don’t just focus on short-term sobriety — we help people build lives they’re proud to live long after they leave our program.

Our Commitment to Lifelong Recovery

Rise Above Recovery was born from a calling — a response to the urgent need for safe, faith-informed, and community-anchored sober living in Charles City and beyond. Our mission goes beyond offering a roof over someone’s head; it’s about offering hope, responsibility, spiritual guidance, and a real path forward.

Residents at Rise Above Recovery live in a space that fosters mutual respect, spiritual growth, and practical preparation for the real world. We provide structure and accountability while encouraging independence and personal transformation. Our model includes:

  • Daily routines including prayer, reflection, and shared responsibilities
  • Faith-based mentorship and connection to local churches
  • Life-skills training (budgeting, cooking, job readiness)
  • One-on-one case management and resident goal tracking
  • Support for co-occurring mental health conditions
  • Job placement partnerships with local businesses and workforce programs
  • Volunteerism and community service to rebuild self-worth and public trust
  • A pathway to aftercare, independent living, or peer leadership

We believe recovery is a lifestyle rooted in purpose and service — and we walk alongside each resident as they rediscover who they were created to be. Whether it's their first attempt at sobriety or their tenth, Rise Above Recovery offers more than a second chance: it offers a family, a future, and faith.
