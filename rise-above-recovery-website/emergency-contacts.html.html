Emergency & Helpline Contacts

Immediate assistance and confidential support from various crisis services and helplines available 24/7.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Immediate confidential support for people in distress or experiencing a crisis. Call or text 988.

Your Life Iowa

24/7 support for mental health, substance abuse, and gambling. Call 855-581-8111 or text 855-895-8398.

Visit Website

National Domestic Violence Hotline

Confidential support for survivors of domestic violence. Call 800-799-7233 or text START to 88788.

Poison Control

Expert guidance in case of poisoning emergencies. Call 800-222-1222.

Iowa Concern Hotline

24-hour stress counseling and crisis support. Call 800-447-1985.

Crisis Text Line

24/7 crisis support via text. Text HOME to 741741.

National Runaway Safeline

Confidential support and resources for runaway youth and their families. Call 800-786-2929.

Veterans Crisis Line

Crisis support for veterans and their families. Call 800-273-8255 and press 1, or text 838255.
